## Why

Operators can quickly lose track of a specific account in the "Other accounts" grid when many cards are present. The sort controls help with ordering, but there is no direct email lookup.

## What Changes

- Add an email search input in the "Other accounts" toolbar.
- Filter displayed "Other accounts" cards by matching account email text.
- Provide email suggestions from known account emails and apply lightweight typo autocorrection on blur.
- Keep existing sort controls and sort behavior unchanged.

## Impact

- Faster account lookup by email without leaving the dashboard.
- Reduced friction when email queries contain minor typos.
